Select Projects

The Effect of ACA Marketplace Policies on People's Costs

This paper estimates the effects of the finalized Affordable Care Act (ACA) marketplace rule and expiration of enhanced tax credits on costs. Cited in litigation that temporarily halted implementation.

People at Risk of Losing Coverage Under Medicaid Work Requirements

This paper estimates the number of people who would have to take action to keep their Medicaid coverage under work requirements enacted in July in the Republican reconciliation legislation.

Medicaid Enrollment Estimates by Congressional District

This resource provides estimates of Medicaid and CHIP enrollment by 119th congressional district. Used on the Hill and featured in various media outlets, including The New York Times.

Simulation of Bayesian Regression Models

My statistics thesis uses simulations to assess the ability of semi-parametric, Bayesian regression models to detect statistical interactions in exposure mixture studies.

Longitudinal Analysis of Spatially Correlated Data

This paper explores the implementation of a multinomial, multilevel logistic regression model for the longitudinal analysis of an unordered categorical response with spatially autocorrelated structure.

Risk and Benefit Modeling of Lung Cancer Screening Uptake

This paper examines the risk and benefit profiles of patients eligible for lung cancer screening and quantifies lost opportunity due to enrollment patterns and eligibility criteria.
